Thaba-bosiu Cultural Village

Experience the Culture of Lesotho

Date:5-12 November 2026

Location: Thaba-Busiu,Lesotho

The Thaba-Bosiu Cultural Village Festival is the most impactfull and special festival in Lesotho. It represent the history of the Basotho Nation, visitor from around the world can come and enjoy the traditional music,food,activities,e.t.c. Thaba-bosiu has a rich history of the Basotho nation,it was the foundation of Basotho.
